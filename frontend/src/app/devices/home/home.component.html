Home

Website for Thesis at UCLouvain that consists of analysing the network traffic of smart home IoT devices to try to predict the energy consumption of the connected IoT devices

1. Setup

First, go to "Setup" and follow the instructions. This consists of searching for new devices on one interface of your network. Then you give them unique names so that you can recognise them. Finally in the setup phase the system will capture the network traffic of your devices while they are idle. (for 6 hours). You can go back to the setup to add new devices when you want.

2. Usage

Once the setup is done the system will be operational. devices should be displayed here on the left, and you can access them to see their events and consumption. You will be asked to label events to help the system to learn to recognise events (such as on or off).

3. Energy consumption

Currently, you need to provide a csv file : total_consumption.csv, that corresponds to the total energy consumption of the smart home to the backend/instance folder. The .csv file should contain 2 columns: the first one, named date, is the timestamps (unix epoch time) and the second one is the corresponding power consumption in Watts. The application will then build consumption profiles for each device.
